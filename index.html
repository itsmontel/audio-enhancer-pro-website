Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 7-day free trial work?
Our 7-day free trial gives you full access to all premium features with no credit card required. After the trial period, you can choose to subscribe for $4.99/month to continue using all the premium features.
Will Audio Enhancer Pro work on any website?
Yes! Audio Enhancer Pro works on virtually any website that plays audio, including YouTube, Netflix, Spotify Web Player, podcast sites, video conferencing platforms, and more. It automatically enhances audio streams across your browser.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription at any time directly from the extension interface. Go to the settings panel and click "Manage Subscription." Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
Does this extension affect my device's performance?
Audio Enhancer Pro is optimized for minimal CPU usage. Our advanced algorithms process audio efficiently in real-time without causing noticeable performance impact on most modern devices.
What is the personalized hearing test?
Our hearing test feature analyzes your hearing capabilities across different frequency ranges and creates a custom EQ profile tailored to your unique hearing pattern. This ensures you hear all audio elements clearly, even those you might normally miss.
Can I use multiple devices with one subscription?
Yes, your subscription is linked to your account, not your device. You can use Audio Enhancer Pro on multiple devices where you're signed into Chrome with the same Google account.
