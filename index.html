Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Audio Enhancer Pro, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information:

  • Usage Data: Audio settings preferences (stored locally on your device only)
  • Technical Data: Browser type and version, installation timestamp, error reports
  • Contact Information: Not collected - we do not currently accept direct contact

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our free service
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Address technical issues and improve user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality

Data Storage and Security

Your audio is processed entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or process any of the actual audio content you listen to. All audio processing happens in your browser using the Web Audio API, and no audio data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Your audio settings and preferences are stored locally in your browser's storage. We do not sync this data to any cloud service. This ensures complete privacy - your audio preferences remain on your device only.

  • Chrome Web Store: For extension distribution (subject to Google's privacy policy)
